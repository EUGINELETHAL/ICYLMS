General Information and Instructions to applicants.

The Leadership and Development Academy (LADA) is established in Africa as a joint initiative of Springdale College UK now known as For Mission College, accredited by Newman University in Birmingham, Africa for SDGs, ICY AFRICA which runs the Springdale Foundation and Alongsiders Online Academy providing training and digital resourses.
This Fellowship comes with a scholarship for tuition which covers a 12- month period for diploma level and 3 years for a professional B.A Degree on leadership and sustainable development, comprising a rigorous training, research and practical application programme on Leadership and sustainable.
All participants must come up with a practical prototype allinged to a specific sustainable development goal(SDGs) to be Implemented at Community level
